About me

I do have a lot of drinks and I do have a lot of plants. I’m a sommelier, mostly self educated, with WSET level 2 and 3 Award in Wines (pass with distinction). I like beverages ranging from wine, beer and ciders and spirits to kombucha and keffir. I dabbled a bit with plants before, but nowadays I kinda live in a jungle.
